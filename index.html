About Us

NayePankh Foundation is a youth-led NGO committed to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, skill development, healthcare awareness, food distribution, women empowerment, and social welfare initiatives. Since its establishment in 2021, the organization has empowered thousands of lives through volunteer-driven projects and community outreach programs across India.
